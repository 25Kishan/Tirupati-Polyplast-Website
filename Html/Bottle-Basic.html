Learn The Industry

What is the difference between a wide mouth packer and a narrow neck cylinder? How is a parison different from a preform? What type of plastic can give me the look of glass? What do all those numbers mean in your Product Catalog?

This section of our web site is designed to help you answer those questions, so it’s easier than ever to find the perfect bottle or jar for your products.
